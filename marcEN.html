Marc Bosch
Business Analyst

As a fourth year Technology and Business student at the Carlos III University in Madrid, Marc represents the perfect profile to understand the value technology can provide companies. He is especially driven to help solve social problems like those tackled by NGOs, with which he has worked in multiple occasions helping develop marketing campaigns, studying funding opportunities and optimizing internal communication. This passion makes him one of the leading voices in terms of social responsibility inside of toolip, a value that is important to us in choosing who to work with and making internal decisions.

Apart from his NGO experience, Marc has taken on different projects during university. These include working for a multinational organization dedicated to market research as Executive Project Assistant, learning the inner workings of a large company; working with a business owner in various projects and learning about entrepreneurship as well as working on the world's largest student social consulting firm.

Marc is currently studying abroad in the University of California, contributing to toolip via media creation and other peripheral tasks.
