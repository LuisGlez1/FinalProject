Daniel Vegara
Data Analyst

Being a tech company that works with non-tech people has the effect of having to come up with inventive solutions for our clients. We don't ask our clients for a technological project, we ask them about their problems and provide them with a solution. This requires creativity, which is given to us by Daniel more than any other member. Apart from his natural creativity, having lived in Madrid, Washington DC and now London gives him a multicultural background that better helps him understand the needs of people with different profiles.

Daniel has completed an degree in Mathematics from Imperial College London and is now completing a Master's degree from the same university. Specializing in Statistics, Probability and Real Analysis, he is specially interested in the area of Data Science. This passion has lead him to work for a data science start up and has made him the ideal companion for Pablo in toolip's development team.

Apart from his dedication to beautiful design, he loves seeing new technologies used in creative ways to solve real world problems. He is also a leading voice in toolip's compromise with social and environmental responsibilities.
